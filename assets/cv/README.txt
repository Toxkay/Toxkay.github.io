Place your CV PDF here as `Toxkay-CV.pdf`.

To add your CV:
1. Export your CV as a PDF.
2. Rename it to `Toxkay-CV.pdf`.
3. Replace this file with your PDF in this directory (`assets/cv/`).

The "Download CV" and "Preview PDF" buttons on the site will automatically point to `assets/cv/Toxkay-CV.pdf`.
